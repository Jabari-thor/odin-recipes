The Trusty, Good and Faithful, Chef's Old Fashioned!!!!

What did the trendy cocktail say to the classic cocktail? "I'm glad that you're Old Fashioned.".

Even if you know nothing about cocktails, chances are you've at least heard of an Old Fashioned.
Made with just four ingredients – whiskey, sugar, Angostura bitters, and an optional (but recommended) orange peel or Luxardo cherry garnish,
the Old Fashioned is up there with other cocktail greats like the Manhattan, Negroni, and Sazerac.

It's super simple to make, as is appropriate for a cocktail designed to highlight its main ingredient – whiskey!

Ingredients:

  • ✔1 sugar cube (1 teaspoon sugar)
  • ✔4 dashes Angostura bitters
  • ✔½ teaspoon water
  • ✔2 ounces (4 tablespoons) bourbon or rye whiskey
  • ✔Orange peel
  • ✔Cocktail cherry, for garnish (optional)
  • ✔Large clear ice cube, for serving

Recipe Instructions:

  1. Place the sugar cube in a lowball glass and add the bitters.

  2. Add the water and mash and swirl it with a muddler or wooden spoon until the sugar is mostly dissolved.

  3. Add the whiskey and swirl to combine. Add a large ice cube.

  4. Use a knife to remove a 1″ wide strip of the orange peel and squeeze the orange peel into the drink to release the oils.

  5. Gently run the peel around the edge of the glass, then place it in the glass.
    If desired, garnish with a cocktail cherry for additional sweetness.

This concludes Chef's Old Fashioned Recipe!
